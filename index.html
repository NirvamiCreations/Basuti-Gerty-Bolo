Basuti Gerty Bolo is the Multi-Awards Winning Founder and CEO of GoToSpace, Owner and Director of GoTo PTY L.T.D Company of Botswana.

Bolo's aims & initiative....

  • Empower women and girls.
  • Reduce gender inequality.
  • Promote women in entrepreneurship.
  • Leadership and use of technology for sustainability.

Basuti Gerty Bolo a Botswana Country and Africa Director for Enabling Extraordinary Entrepreneurship (E2E), UK;A space4women mentor and ambassador; endowed chair of educational technologies; Botswana CountrySpace science and technology chair; Director for MobileStudies Africa; Science Technology, Engineering, Mathematics (STEM) promoter, mentor and advisor;Women in technology and Global Women for Good ambassador.

Basuti's Aim

For Space Enthusiastic.

01

Bring Space Light to the Community

To educate, train, mentor and empower more than 1000 000 (youth, women and Girls) and the communities with the knowledge of Space Science, technology, and applications for sustainable development by 2030.

02

Vision

To promote Space Science Technology, Engineering, Mathematics (STEM) & ARTS by training and mentoring women and girls on Space science and STEM to reduce gender inequality on STEM sector.

03

Mission

To drive Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) by providing training on space science, technology and applications on Management, Education, Research, Innovation to monitor and manage resources for sustainable resources.
